# Grind Size

Grind size is the single most influential variable in espresso. It sets the surface area exposed to water and the resistance the puck offers to flow. Too coarse and the shot runs fast and sour; too fine and it chokes the machine and turns bitter.

[[espresso-extraction]] `depends_on` grind size more than any other input. Grind is `related_to` [[tamping]], since both shape how evenly water moves through the bed, and [[dose-ratio]] `depends_on` grind because the dose and grind must be tuned together to hit a target yield.
