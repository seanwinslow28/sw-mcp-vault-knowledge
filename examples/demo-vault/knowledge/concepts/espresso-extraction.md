# Espresso Extraction

Espresso extraction is the process of forcing hot water through a compacted bed of finely ground coffee under pressure to produce a concentrated shot. The result depends on the interaction of several variables rather than any single one.

The extraction quality rests on [[grind-size]] above all, and is enabled by [[pump-pressure]], [[tamping]], and [[water-chemistry]]. Dialing in a shot means adjusting these against each other until the [[flavor-extraction-stages]] land in the balanced window.
